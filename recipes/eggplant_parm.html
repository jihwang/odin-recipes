Eggplant Parm 🍆

Author

Chinese Cooking Demystified

Ingredients

  • Large Eggplant (2 lbs) - sliced ½” thick
  • Olive Oil (¼ cup)
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper
  • Onion (1, small) - thickly sliced
  • Garlic (4 cloves) - thinly sliced
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Anchovy (4 fillets)
  • Whole Tomatoes (28 oz. can)
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper
  • Panko bread crumbs (¾ cup)
  • Olive Oil (¼ cup)
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground black pepper
  • Grated parmesan (⅓ cup)
  • Capers (2–3 tbsp) - chopped
  • Fresh Oregano (2 tbsp) OR Dry (1 tbsp)
  • Parsley (⅓ cup) - coarsely chopped
  • Fresh Mozzarella (8 oz.) - thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°, drizzle cup olive oil, roast 25–30 minutes
  2. Heat two tablespoons of olive oil in a medium pot over medium-high heat.
  3. Add onion and garlic, season with salt and pepper, cook 8–10 minutes.
  4. Add crushed red pepper flakes and anchovies until melted.
  5. Pour the juices from the tomatoes into the pot, crush the tomatoes with your hands into the pot.
  6. Salt and pepper and let it simmer gently for 15–30 minutes.
  7. Set half aside and freeze or refrigerate the rest.
  8. Heat the remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil in a small to medium skillet over medium heat.
  9. Add the breadcrumbs and season with salt and pepper. Brown, 5–7 minutes.
  10. Assemble (in a 1 qt baking dish OR 6” skillet)
  11. ½ of the tomato sauce
  12. ½ of the Eggplant
  13. ½ (Parm + parsley + capers + oregano)
  14. ½ bread crumb
  15. ½ mozzarella
  16. Repeat
  17. Preheat oven to 450°, bake 15–20 min
